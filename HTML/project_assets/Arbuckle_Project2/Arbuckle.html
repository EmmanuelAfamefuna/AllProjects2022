About Jon Arbuckle

Hi there everyone! My name is Jon Arbuckle. I am 30 years old and I am a cartoonist that lives on earth. Although my pets drives me crazy, I can't imagine life without them.

I spend about half my time going to the grocery stores to buy enough foods to feed Garfield. I like marinara sauce, frozen pizzas, and lots and lots of lasanga. When I come home, Garfield usually comes seconds, and I had to go back to the store to buy more food. I wasn't too happy about that.
